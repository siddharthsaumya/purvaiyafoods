Our Founder's Story

Born in Delhi to a family of spice traders, our founder grew up surrounded by aromas and flavors. After a decade working in hospitality, they realized something was missing in the market - truly authentic, unadulterated spice blends.

"My grandmother's recipes were the inspiration. She never compromised on quality, and neither do we," says the founder, who spent years traveling to spice-growing regions to perfect their craft.

Our Journey

2018

Brand Launch

Started with three signature blends from a small kitchen in Delhi. First orders came from friends and family.

2019

First Restaurant Partnership

A leading restaurant discovered our blends and made us their exclusive spice supplier. Recognition from chefs followed.

2020

Moved to Dedicated Facility

Expanded to a proper production facility with dedicated roasting and grinding equipment to scale operations.

2021-2023

Regional Expansion

Expanded collection to 25+ blends, partnered with 50+ restaurants, and grew online customer base to thousands.

2024

National Recognition

Today, we serve thousands of home cooks and restaurants across India, committed to bringing authentic flavors to every table.
