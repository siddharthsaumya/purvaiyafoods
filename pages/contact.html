Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

We offer standard shipping (3-5 business days) and express shipping (1-2 business days). Orders are processed within 24 hours.

Are your spices organic?

While not all are certified organic, we source from farms that practice sustainable and chemical-free cultivation. No additives or fillers are used.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact us for a full refund or replacement.

Can I use these spices for dietary restrictions?

All our blends are vegan, gluten-free, and nut-free. However, check individual product pages for specific allergen information.

How should I store my spices?

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Our glass jars help maintain freshness. Properly stored, our blends stay fresh for 12-18 months.
